Geohorizon

To say a perfect sentence about Geohorizon, it must be like this: “The enlarging vision of new hopes and performance to take Geomatics society one step beyond by its successful trend all over the years”

The much-awaited event of the Geoinformatics society lives up to the above verse. It never makes you rest even for a single dull moment. With greater challenges, innovative themes, and extraordinary events Geohorizon is sure to quench your thirst for knowledge and excitement. This event which takes place in the month of March is one of the revelation towards the advancements of Geospatial technology. Students from all over the country make their enthusiastic march to the arena on March to be a part of the prestigious event.

Visit Site


The interesting line up of events, guest lectures of renowned pioneers of the geospatial field, innovative workshops and so on keeps Geohorizon a memorable and exquisite event for all of us. All the contests show an outburst of knowledge and sound technicality of the participant and their work.

Paper presentation

The ultimate show which highlights the bloom of advancements in the field of Geoinformatics. From neural networks to disaster management, the topics cover a wide arena for the participants to research, develop, present and win.

Poster presentation

Here the poster speaks volumes on powerful topics and their technicalities.

Guest lecture

This piece of Geohorizon is the knowledge-sharing component. Here renowned guests who are the pioneers in the upcoming fields of Geospatial technology present an informative lecture to empower the young-minds.

Workshops

Be it matlab or GPS, the workshops makes you a master of them. From lab to field, you have hands-on experience to widen your knowledge and skills through expert guidance.

Quiz

This is the mind-blowing event of the Geohorizon. If you think the answer resides in your mind but struggling to find a voice, then this is your destination.

Visual instincts

Eyes are the human sensors. So this event is the chance for the powerful sensor to spot out the answer on its own.

“Get excited and enthusiastic about you own dream. This excitement is like a forest fire-you can smell it, taste it, and see it from a mile away……….” Geohorizon is a special event for everyone who craves for fun in learning experience.
