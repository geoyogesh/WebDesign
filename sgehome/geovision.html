GEOVISION

SGE publishes a monthly a issue of its activities through its newsletter GEOVISION. This monthly newsletter reaches all geospatial arena starting from academic organisations through companies to keep them all posted about the happenings at IRS.

"It is not the strongest of the species that survive, nor the most intelligent, but the one most responsive to change."- Charles Darwin

Keeping up with the quote, the Society of Geo informatics Engineers have put forth many new activities. It is abuzz with new activities, responding to this dynamic ever changing world. One such new change is Geovision, the e-newsletter.

Geovision is a one of the kind e-newsletter published by SGE- the Society of Geoinformatics Engineers. The SGE newsletter is a new inception and its main objectives being an effective platform to showcase the student’s activities, their achievements and their innovations in the direction of the current technology. The newsletter serves as an efficient bridge between the student community and the industrial community, thus eliminating the gap between them. The newsletter is set to trigger the inspiration of the student community and motivate them to bring about a revolution in the field of Geomatics.

Some of the regular features of the newsletter are

  • Overviews of society activities
  • Reports on internships undertaken
  • A quick glance through students projects
  • Students achievements
  • Alumni talk
  • Latest development in the field of Geomatics
  • Tidbits about the current affairs
