Welcome!

Society of GeoInformatics Engineers (SGE) exclusively for Geo-informatics engineers inaugurated by Dr.Navalgund, Director NRSA, Hyderabad on 30th September, 2004 in lieu of the student community, to help them take part in for the betterment of their understanding the various subjects of Geomatics and to work for the Geomatic Community as a whole.

At SGE we conduct regular weekly activities to ensure sharing of ideas on various subjects of GeoInformatics. SGE also intended the increase of participation between the students and the corporate community. It enlightened students as to what the corporate community expected form them.

The Society also conducts regular events and lectures by eminent persons of the geocommunity for the betterment of the students. The Society is the first student organization of this kind pursuing excellence in the Geomatics Community.
